The name for the movie "Tron" comes from the "Trace On" command, which was used to track the lines of code being executed. Must have been especially useful when you had GOTO commands scattered all over the place.

Source: Reddit [episode discussion thread](https://old.reddit.com/r/HaltAndCatchFire/comments/70khec/halt_and_catch_fire_4x05_nowhere_man_episode/dna5qjf/?context=3#dna5qjf) of the TV show [Halt and Catch Fire](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt2543312/).
